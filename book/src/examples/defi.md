# DeFi Examples

Production-ready Soroban smart contract examples covering the most common Decentralized Finance primitives — from basic token swaps to liquidity mining and yield vaults.

| 07 | [Staking Pool](./defi/07-staking-pool.md) | Staking with time-weighted reward accumulator | Intermediate |
| 08 | [Liquidity Mining](./defi/08-liquidity-mining.md) | Multi-pool mining with allocation points | Advanced |
